The Manunggul Jar

The Manunggul Jar is a burial vessel dating back to the Neolithic period. The jar's craftmanship and symbolism reflect early Filipino's spiritual beliefs and reverence for the dead.

The Warka Vase

The Warka Vase is a significant artifact from ancient Mesopotamia. Its carved reliefs depict religious rituals and offerings, revealing insights into the spiritual practices of early civilizations.

Greek Amphora

The Greek Amphora is a symbol of classical pottery, often used to store oil, wine, or grain. Its intricate designs, depicting mythology and athletic feats, showcase the artistry of ancient Greece.
